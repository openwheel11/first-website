My Favorite Cars

Classics

First, there are the classic cars. Typically they are sports cars, but not always.

The top of the list have to be Ferraris. Here are some of my favorites:

1967 Ferrari 330 2+2

I purchased one of these after college and restored it. Boy was it a learning experience. Good thing I had access to a machine shop and a friend who was a very talented machinist and mechanic. Parts were difficult to impossible to come by; we ended up making many of the parts ourselves.

1970's Ferrari 308 GTB

This is the classic 308 GTB, made famous by the TV Show "Magnum PI" Note that it is the "European Model" which I beleive is much better looking than the US model.

Off Road 4x4 Trucks

Next up are the 4x4's. I have many happy memories of off-roading with my family, camping in the desert and exploring the wilderness. One of the first SUV's (before that was even a term) was this one:

1972 Chevy Blazer
